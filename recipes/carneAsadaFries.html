Carne Asada Fries

Loaded Carne Asada Fries

Ingredients:

    Carne Asada
  • 2 pounds of flank steak

    • Marinade
  • 3 limes squeezed
  • 1 orange squeezed
  • 1 bunch of cilantro
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • A whole head of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • 1-3 tbsp of neutral oil (I used avocado)

    • Fries
  • (All of the seasonings are optional you can just use the fries out of the bag)
  • A bag of Frozen fries
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 1 tbsp of pepper
  • 1 tbsp of MSG
  • 1 tsp of cayenne powder
  • 1 tsp lemon pepper
  • salt to taste
  • shredded monterey jack cheese

    • Guacamole
  • 3 avocados ripe
  • 1/2 red onion diced
  • 1 Roma tomatoes diced
  • 2 tbsp of cilantro diced
  • 1 jalapeno diced
  • 1 lime juiced
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Blend Carne Asada Marinade ingredients together

  2. Pour over Beef and Marinate for at least 30 minutes or a couple of hours

  3. While marinating, make your guacamole,

  4. Sear the beef in a pan for 2-3 minutes per side on high. Or you can grill it as well. While the beef is cooking, air fry your fries according to the package directions

  5. Rest Beef for 10 minutes, while the beef is resting add your finished fries into a bowl and add spices and mix it up

  6. Add fries on an oven-safe plate, then add your cheese. Broil till cheese is melted. Then add the sliced carne asada on top all pretty, then a dollop of guacamole, and then add sour cream drizzle over top
